FAQs
What’s an NFT?

An NFT is a ‘non-fungible token’, which is a one-of-a-kind ownable digital asset that you can virtually collect or trade. When you puchase one of these NFTs, you are the official owner of an original William White piece. Those are some serious bragging rights.

Do I need cryptocurrency to buy a William White NFT?

Not at all. Right now, we are accepting USD which you can pay through PayPal, Apple Pay, or your everyday credit card. Eventually, we will be minting the purchased NFTs, which is a crypto term meaning that your NFT will become a part of the Ethereum blockchain – the cryptocurrency world where the vast majority of NFTs are held so that your NFT will become tradeable.

What happens when I buy one of the NFTs?

You’ll be sent the digital collectable to your e-mail you provide right away. Store it on your phone or keep it on your computer and proudly show it off to the William White fan community! Once your NFT is minted, it will also show up in your Ethereum blockchain along with all of your other NFT purchases and trades.

If you’re one of the lucky ones who received an unlockable, you’ll also be gifted the real-world prizes linked to certain NFT purchases – including a personal Zoom call with Will himself! *Swoon*

Does this mean I’m now a part of a new William White community?

You bet! You’ll get exclusive first access to all of William’s future NFT drops, which we can promise will only get bigger, better, more exciting, and more personal. Welcome to the metaverse... sorry... the Willverse. We’re so happy you’re here with us.
